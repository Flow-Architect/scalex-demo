# Code of Conduct

ScaleX welcomes constructive, respectful collaboration.

Contributors should:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Keep discussion focused on the project.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Handle security concerns privately according to `SECURITY.md`.
- Avoid sharing secrets, real customer data, private logs, or live credentials in issues or pull
  requests.

Maintainers may remove disruptive content or block participation that puts people, data, or the
project at risk.
